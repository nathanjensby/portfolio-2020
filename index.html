myUsual

list sharing

Everyone has grocery lists. Whether it be generic grocery lists, preferred foods/drinks lists, or special occasions lists...Everyone makes them.

Say you're going to grab items that are on your friend's list. But what if they forgot something on that list, wanted to change the list, or simply wanted to create a list on the fly while they're driving home from work? This can easily turn into a horrid and convoluted experience. No one wants to deal with multiple texts, notes, emails, etc of obtaining those changes or creations.

That's where myUsual simplifies your life. myUsual is THE sleek and simple way of creating, editing, and most importantly, sharing YOUR USUAL items and lists.

Made with Auth0, Express.js, Heroku, MongoDB, and Node.js
